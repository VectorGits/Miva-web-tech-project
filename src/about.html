About Us

Tulos is a unique fashion completely revolutionizing the casual and corporate ware industry. We started out as 4 young minds seeking to make an impact in the world using fashion. Each of us connected on our love for the simple things of life. Most of us preferred wearing colors like black, white, grey and beige. People often wondered why we were stingy with adding extra colours to our attire and we simply explained “There’s a sexiness to simplicity” and that is how our motto was born. While our designs may look simple, there’s often a certain level of charisma one attains by becoming one with simplicity. We create styles for individuals who like to be quiet in the room but are immediately noticed when they walk into one. We have dedicated our lives and time to giving our customers the best service, style and quality at an affordable price.

Vision Statement

To give confidence to those who prefer to be color blind

Mission Statement

To paint the world black, white and beige

FAQ

What is Tulos?

Tulos is a fashion brand that offers stylish, high-quality clothing and accessories for men and women. We pride ourselves on our unique designs and commitment to sustainability.

How can I place an order?

You can place an order directly through our website. Browse our collections, select the items you like, add them to your cart, and follow the checkout process. If you have any questions, our customer service team is here to help.

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept various payment methods including major credit cards (Visa, MasterCard, American Express), PayPal, and other secure payment gateways. All transactions are processed securely.

Do you offer international shipping?

Yes, Tulos offers international shipping to many countries. Shipping fees and delivery times vary depending on your location. Please check our shipping policy page for more details.

How long will it take to receive my order?

Delivery times vary based on your location and the shipping method selected. Typically, orders within the country are delivered within 3-5 business days, while international orders may take 7-14 business days. We provide tracking information for all shipments.

Do you have physical stores?

Currently, Tulos operates primarily online. However, we participate in various pop-up events and markets where you can shop our collections in person. Follow us on social media or sign up for our newsletter to stay updated on our events.

What is your return policy?

We offer a 30-day return policy. If you are not completely satisfied with your purchase, you can return it within 30 days of receipt for a full refund or exchange. Items must be unworn, unwashed, and in their original condition with all tags attached. Please visit our returns page for more information.
