About Us

Tulos is a unique fashion completely revolutionizing the casual and corporate ware industry. We started out as 4 young minds seeking to make an impact in the world using fashion. Each of us connected on our love for the simple things of life. Most of us preferred wearing colors like black, white, grey and beige. People often wondered why we were stingy with adding extra colours to our attire and we simply explained “There’s a sexiness to simplicity” and that is how our motto was born. While our designs may look simple, there’s often a certain level of charisma one attains by becoming one with simplicity. We create styles for individuals who like to be quiet in the room but are immediately noticed when they walk into one. We have dedicated our lives and time to giving our customers the best service, style and quality at an affordable price.

Vision Statement

To give confidence to those who prefer to be color blind
